At Tech Ninja, our major service is the provision of free, safe and secure modified APKs. We usually try to avoid crowding our database with apps that aren't in demand so we source our APKs from two major sources. 
1. Store Statistics: The Play store statistics for regularly downloaded games and apps is one of the places we go to when trying to get games and apps to populate our database.
2. You: Yes you. Tech Ninja is primarily designed to be used by you and such the majority of the apps and games you will actually find here are the ones that were requested by you the user. We always try to handle every single request so make a request today!

Now bear in mind that in as much as we try to handle all your APK mod requests, there're some apps and even games that require special attention. When we see a request like someone asks to create a mod for Amazon Store... Therefore all APKs you request to be modified have to be reviewed before they can proceed to be indexed.

So, indexing is basically submitting the app's name to our AI Database, CRUNCH, what CRUNCH will do is ideally save the APKs on it's database. This database is also available for our third party affiliates to look at the interact to.

After it is indexed, our third party affiliates receive this APK and then make the necessary altercations and then send it back to our database. Now immediately a new APK is sent to our database, CRUNCH will check if that AP is what someone has previously requested and if yes, CRUNCH will automatically send a mail to the person informing that their request went through.

Your security and privacy are our top privacy at APK  Hunter and as such before we make mod APKs available to you we try to scan them with our scanning algorithms and they have to meet certain standards before we can upload them to the mod store, if errors are detected, we have our developers who can look at the issue and try to resolve them.

In the absence of any issues or errors, we will send that mod APK to our database again and it will push it to the mod store where it can be accessible for download. In addition CRUNCH may also add the app's card as a "New Release" on the website.
