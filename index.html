Frequently Asked Questions

Are mod apps free?

Yes! On Apk Hunter you get access to modded apps completely free of charge.

Who makes the modded apks?

As stated in our terms of service, Apk hunter is a Drone. Ideally, we created an AI model that seeks the modded apks from our affiliates and then our team of experienced developers will check the apps that our AI sends to ensure conformity with our standards.

How are the apks updated?

Usually we inform about updates to apps via our Telegram Channel. As a drone, we don't have control as to when our third party affiliates will release updates to an app, usually they release updates about 1 week after the original is uploaded to Play Store. And immediately they release an update, it usually prompts you in the app and we update our database accordingly.

Some apps in the app store have no download button?

If an app in the app store has no download button. It could be as a result of one of four things.
(1. The app is new to the database and is still undergoing security and standards checks.
(2. The app is long overdue for an update and our affiliates are yet to update and this has caused performance issues on the app.
(3. The original developers have reached out to ask their app be removed.
(4. The app has too much bugs and reports.
